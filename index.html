About Me

Some things you should know: I would give everything I own to high five Drew Barrymore or Dolly Parton and I will die on the hill that soft drinks should be called SODA (I hope this doesn't disqualify me from your next project, "Pop" enthusiasts).

When I'm not designing, you can find me kicking butt at marketing and photography. I am someone who you can count on to show up and get the job done. I make it a priority to learn all I can to improve what I know and what I can offer my client.

With over 6 years of experience in graphic design and marketing, I've achieved honors such as State Champion Advertising Designer for SkillsUSA and am president of American Institute of Graphic Arts Springfield, MO Chapter. I live for creating a positive difference and strive for an inclusive community and culture.

But, that's enough about me. Let's get to know you!
